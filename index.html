Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full detailing service take?

A full detailing service typically takes between 4-8 hours, depending on the size of the vehicle and the level of detailing required. For more extensive services like paint correction or ceramic coating, it may take longer.

How often should I get my car detailed?

We recommend getting your car detailed every 4-6 months to maintain its appearance and protect the paint. However, this can vary based on your driving habits, environment, and how well you maintain your vehicle between detailing sessions.

What's the difference between waxing and ceramic coating?

Waxing provides a temporary layer of protection that typically lasts 1-3 months. Ceramic coating, on the other hand, bonds with the paint at a molecular level, offering superior protection against UV rays, chemicals, and minor scratches for 2-5 years.
